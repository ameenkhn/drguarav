FAQs

Answers To Common Questions

How do I book an appointment?
Appointments are confirmed in advance over the phone or WhatsApp. Call +91 95413 51575 to book an in-person or online consultation slot.
What are the clinic consultation timings?
In-person: Monday to Wednesday and Friday & Saturday, 2:30 PM to 7:30 PM; Sunday, 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Thursday is the weekly off. Online consultation is available daily from 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM.
Do you offer online / video consultations?
Yes. Online consultation slots are available every day from 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM. Call or WhatsApp in advance to book and confirm your slot.
Which conditions does the clinic treat?
Type 1, Type 2 and gestational diabetes, obesity, overweight and metabolic syndrome, diabetic neuropathy and diabetic foot complications, and general lifestyle and endocrine disorders.
What tests and diagnostics are available in-house?
A complete range of blood tests, HbA1c, body composition analysis (BMC/BCA), neurostim & neuropathy tests, diabetic foot assessment, spirometry, hand-grip strength and ECG — all under one roof, with an in-clinic pharmacy.
Do you treat all age groups?
Yes. The clinic welcomes patients of all ages — children, young adults and elderly patients — with no upper or lower age limit.
What should I bring for my first consultation?
Please bring previous prescriptions, recent sugar and lab reports, any scans, your glucose-monitoring logs and a list of your current medications.
How does the weight-management programme work?
Obesity care is physician-supervised and evidence-based — beginning with body composition analysis, followed by a personalised diet chart, IV drips/therapies where appropriate, and regular progress monitoring.
