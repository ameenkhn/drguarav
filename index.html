About The Doctor

Dr. Gaurav K Kesar — Diabetologist & Obesity Expert

Director & Consulting Diabetologist at MediHub, Diabetes & Obesity Care, Jammu, Dr. Gaurav K Kesar brings 17+ years of clinical experience in diabetology and general medicine, caring for both rural and urban patients from across J&K. His areas of interest are clinical diabetology, obesity and diabetic foot care.

His approach is evidence-based and patient-first — combining comprehensive diabetes management, advanced obesity care, an in-house laboratory, pharmacy and modern diagnostics so every patient receives complete, convenient care under one roof.
