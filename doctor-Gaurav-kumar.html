About Dr. Gaurav K Kesar

Dr. Gaurav K Kesar is a Consultant Diabetologist and Obesity Expert with 17+ years of clinical experience in diabetology and general medicine. As Director of MediHub, Diabetes & Obesity Care in Jammu, he provides evidence-based, patient-first care across clinical diabetology, advanced obesity management and diabetic foot care.

His areas of interest are clinical diabetology, obesity and diabetic foot care. Over 17 years he has treated both rural and urban diabetic patients from all districts of Jammu & Kashmir, and previously served as Consultant & Head of the Department of Diabetology at GB Pant Hospital, Jammu (2016–2020).

He combines international training — fellowships and certifications from the UK, USA and a SCOPE certification from the World Obesity Federation — with a complete in-house setup of laboratory, pharmacy and modern diagnostics, so every patient receives accurate diagnosis and end-to-end care under one roof.
